Lesson 7: Ethical Hacking Practice Labs

Ethical hacking skills must be practiced in legal and safe environments. Practicing on real websites without permission is illegal.

Never hack random websites, government systems, or banks.

Why Practice Labs Matter

Labs are intentionally vulnerable systems created for learning. They allow you to practice without breaking the law.

Recommended Legal Platforms

TryHackMe
Hack The Box Academy
PortSwigger Web Security Academy
OWASP Juice Shop

Local Practice (Your Own System)

You can safely practice on your own computer.

localhost
Virtual machines
Test applications
