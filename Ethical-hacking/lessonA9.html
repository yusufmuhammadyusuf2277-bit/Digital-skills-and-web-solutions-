Lesson A9: Red Team vs Blue Team

Advanced cybersecurity involves both attackers and defenders.

Red Team (Attack Simulation)

Simulates real attackers
Finds hidden weaknesses
Tests detection systems

Blue Team (Defense)

Monitors systems
Detects intrusions
Responds to incidents

Purple Team

Collaboration
Knowledge sharing
Improved security posture
Professionals often switch between roles.
