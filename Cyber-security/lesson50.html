Lesson 50: Cybersecurity Strategy, Compliance & Leadership

Advanced cybersecurity involves strategy, leadership, and compliance. Leaders plan security programs, ensure legal/regulatory compliance, and guide teams in defending assets.

Example:
A CISO sets policies, approves security tools, trains teams, and ensures GDPR/ISO compliance across the organization.
Exercise: Draft a one-page cybersecurity policy for a small company, covering passwords, incident reporting, and cloud security.
