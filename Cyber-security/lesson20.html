Lesson 20: Cybersecurity Careers

Cybersecurity offers many career paths. Skills you learn here can lead to professional jobs defending networks and systems.

  • SOC Analyst – Monitors security alerts 24/7
  • Security Engineer – Designs secure systems
  • Penetration Tester – Finds vulnerabilities ethically
  • Digital Forensics Expert – Investigates attacks
Example:
A SOC analyst notices multiple failed login attempts and blocks the IP before any damage occurs.
