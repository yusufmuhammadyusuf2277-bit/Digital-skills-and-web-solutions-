Lesson 30: Cybersecurity Mindset

A strong cybersecurity mindset is proactive, curious, and disciplined. Think like an attacker to anticipate threats, follow best practices, and continuously learn.

Example:
Regularly updating your skills, reviewing system logs, and thinking ahead about potential threats shows a cybersecurity mindset.

Always stay vigilant, never assume systems are perfectly secure, and treat security as part of your daily work.
