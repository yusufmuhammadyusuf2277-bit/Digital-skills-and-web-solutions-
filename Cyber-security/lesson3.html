Lesson 3: Common Cyber Threats

Cyber threats are methods attackers use to harm systems or steal information. Understanding them helps defenders prevent attacks.

  • Malware: Harmful software like viruses or ransomware.
  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages that trick users.
  • Password attacks: Guessing or stealing passwords.
Example:
An email pretending to be from your bank asking you to “verify” your account.
