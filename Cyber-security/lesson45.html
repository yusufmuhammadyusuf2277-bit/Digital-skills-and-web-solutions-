Lesson 45: Bug Bounty Programs & Ethical Hacking Careers

Bug bounty programs let ethical hackers find vulnerabilities and earn rewards. Careers in cybersecurity range from SOC analysts to penetration testers.

Example:
A hacker reports a security flaw to a company and receives $500 through their bug bounty program.
Exercise: Sign up for a legal bug bounty platform like HackerOne or Bugcrowd and explore open programs.
