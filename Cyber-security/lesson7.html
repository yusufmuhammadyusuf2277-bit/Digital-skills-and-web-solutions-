Lesson 7: Passwords & Authentication

Authentication is the process of verifying who you are. Weak passwords are one of the most common security problems.

  • Use long, unique passwords for every account.
  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) whenever possible.
  • Never share passwords with anyone.
Example:
Logging in with your password and a code sent to your phone is MFA, making your account harder to hack.
