Lesson 47: The Hacker Mindset

Ethical hackers think like attackers to protect systems. Key traits include:

  • Curiosity – always asking “what if?”
  • Problem-solving – finding solutions to complex issues
  • Persistence – not giving up easily
  • Attention to detail – small mistakes can be exploited
  • Ethics – responsibility and legality first
