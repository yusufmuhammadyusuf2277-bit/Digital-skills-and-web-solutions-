Lesson 46: What is Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking is legally testing systems, networks, or applications to identify security weaknesses before attackers do.

  • Also called “White Hat Hacking”
  • Legal and authorized
  • Helps organizations strengthen security
  • Relies on knowledge, tools, and methodology

⚠️ Never hack without permission. Unauthorized hacking is illegal.
