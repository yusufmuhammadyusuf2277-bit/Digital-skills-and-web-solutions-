Lesson 41: What is Vulnerability Scanning

Vulnerability scanning is the process of identifying security weaknesses in systems, applications, or networks. It helps organizations fix problems before attackers exploit them.

  • Detect outdated software
  • Identify misconfigurations
  • Check for weak passwords
  • Scan your own devices safely

⚠️ Only scan systems you own or have permission to scan.
